Upload Progress Photos

Review these tips, then continue to select wound locations.

Upload clear images of your wounds so you can track healing over time and share updates with your care team.

Tips for best results:

  • Use good, even lighting — avoid harsh shadows
  • Hold your camera 6–12 inches from the wound
  • Include a size reference (coin, ruler) if possible
  • Keep the wound centered in the frame
  • Take multiple angles for the same wound
Accepted formats: JPEG, PNG, HEIC/HEIF, WebP
Max file size: 5 MB per image
